About Energy Bar Chronicles

Our Mission

Founded in 2023 following the first documented Tesla drone incident in Cape May, Energy Bar Chronicles serves as both a documentation center and solution hub for coastal communities affected by autonomous drone navigation errors.

We specialize in developing and sharing recipes for specialized energy bars that incorporate electromagnetic-dampening ingredients, helping protect beachfront properties from unwanted drone attention.

The Tesla Drone Phenomenon

Since Tesla's rollout of autonomous delivery drones in 2023, we've documented over 247 incidents of drones mistaking Victorian-style beach houses for charging stations. Our community-driven research has led to innovative dietary solutions.
